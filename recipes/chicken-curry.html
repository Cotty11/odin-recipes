Chicken Curry

Description

This simple chicken curry recipe is easy to make and tastes delicious. The ingredients are easy to source and can be found quite cheap in most supermarkets.

This recipe can be cooked and stored in the fridge so that you can enjoy this delicious chicken curry for a second day. It can be enjoyed with rice or chips, and don't forget to have some naan breads to accompany it.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • Fresh gineger (3cm)
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 500ml Coconut Milk
  • 500ml Hot water
  • 2 OXO chicken stock cubes
  • 1 cup Frozen Peas
  • 7 tablesspoons Korma curry powder
  • 3 tablespoons Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breasts into bite sized pieces
  2. Heat olive oil on medium/high and add garlic, ginger and chicken when oil is warm
  3. Brown the chicken until all pieces are sealed and white in colour
  4. Add curry powder, stir and leave for 2 minutes
  5. Add the chicken stock and coconut milk
  6. Bring to the boil and simmer for 50 minutes
  7. Add froxen peas around 10 minutes before serving
